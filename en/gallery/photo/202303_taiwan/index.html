A visit to MiDAS Observatory in Qixingtan Beach (Hualien County)

We visited MiDAS (Milun Fault Drilling and All-inclusive Sensing project) observatory in Hualien to learn about the distributed acoustic sensing．

MiDAS Observatory

Qixingtan Beach

Qixingtan Beach is the beautiful arc-shaped coast with 15 km long. We can observe the surface displacement by the Milun Fault. MiDAS observatory is located in front of the Qixingtan Beach.

I was really satisfied with this visit and acknowledge their kind support by the MiDAS group led by Prof. Kuo-Fong Ma . Our photos for the visit are also exhibited in the E-DREaM Homepage (Earthquake Disaster & Risk Evaluation and Management Center, National Central University).
